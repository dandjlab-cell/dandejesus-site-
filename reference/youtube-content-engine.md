## Key Strategic Initiatives
1. **YouTube Shorts Pipeline:** Took insights from TikTok and other platforms, turned them into YouTube Shorts. Shorts served as a testing ground — top performers informed which topics got full-length house tour episodes.
2. **International Tours:** Pushed for international house tours (Paris, etc.). During the French tours period, France spiked to 18.6% of views (US was at 14% during that window). France was NOT the #1 geography overall — but tapping this new European audience drove some of the channel's largest subscriber growth surges. This was a strategic initiative, not organic.
3. **Content System:** Built a repeatable pipeline — Shorts → test → full tour. Scaled from individual video production to a systematic content operation.
